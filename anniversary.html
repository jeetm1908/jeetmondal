A love letter

My dearest Bubu,

Two years. It still feels like we're just at the beginning of something infinite — something neither of us could have imagined before we found each other. Every morning that I wake up knowing you exist in my world feels like a quiet kind of miracle.

You have this way of making even the smallest moments feel like they matter. A shared laugh over nothing. The warmth of your presence in an ordinary room. You've taught me that love isn't always grand gestures — it's the steady, gentle choosing of each other, every single day.

I think about how much we've grown together. The conversations that lasted till the stars tired out. The way you hold space for me when I need it most. You are my favourite chapter, my safest place, and my greatest adventure all at once.

Two years is just the prologue. I want to keep writing this story with you — through all the seasons, all the ordinary Tuesdays, and every extraordinary moment in between. You are, and always will be, my favourite person.

Happy anniversary, my love. Thank you for being you. Thank you for us.

Forever yours, Duggu 🤍
